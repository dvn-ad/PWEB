Tourist Places

Here are some tourist places that you can visit in Bekasi:

Summarecon Mall Bekasi

Summarecon Mall Bekasi (also known as SMB) is a shopping and entertainment center in Bekasi, West Java, Indonesia. The mall opened on June 28, 2013, and has a retail area of over 80,000 m².

Pakuwon Mall Bekasi

Pakuwon Mall Bekasi is a shopping center located in South Bekasi District, Bekasi City, West Java. Established in 2024, this mall was developed by Pakuwon Jati, a property company from Surabaya City, East Java, which was previously known for managing a number of superblocks in Indonesia. Pakuwon Mall Bekasi is their 4th mall with the title "Pakuwon Mall" and the only one in the western part of Indonesia after Pakuwon City Mall and Pakuwon Mall Surabaya in Surabaya City, East Java, Pakuwon Mall Solo Baru in Sukoharjo, Central Java, and Pakuwon Mall Jogja in Sleman, Yogyakarta. Pakuwon Mall Bekasi occupies the Pakuwon Bekasi superblock, a mixed-use area planned to include an apartment and two star hotels under the management of Marriott International through a franchise agreement.Pakuwon Bekasi Superblock is also the third mixed-use area in the western part of Indonesia after Gandaria City and Kota Kasablanka.

Grand Galaxy Park

Grand Galaxy Park (abbreviated as GGP) is a three-story retail located within the Grand Galaxy City residential area in Bekasi City, West Java with easy access via the Jakarta Outer Ring Road and the Jakarta-Cikampek Toll Road, with unique outdoor areas namely the Alfresco Dining Area and Festival District and Function Hall located on the 2nd floor.

Grand Metropolitan Mall

Grand Metropolitan Mall is a shopping and entertainment center in Bekasi, Indonesia. This mall opened on July 12, 2013 (age 12). and is a continuation of Metropolitan Mall Bekasi which has been present here 20 years earlier. The shopping center, designed by the United States consultant, Development Design Group, has a retail area of approximately 52,443 m².This mall will also be integrated with apartments and offices, M. Gold Tower.
